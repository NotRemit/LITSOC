About Us

Welcome to the Literary Society of VSSUT (LITSOC), where words come alive and creativity knows no bounds. We are a vibrant community of writers, poets, and literature enthusiasts dedicated to fostering literary excellence.

"A mind needs books as a sword needs a whetstone, if it is to keep its edge."

- Tyrion Lannister

Through our events, workshops, and publications, we provide a platform for budding writers to showcase their talent and engage in meaningful literary discussions.
